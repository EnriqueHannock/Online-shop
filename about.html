About Nashe Designs

Nashe Designs is a modern fashion and tailoring house based in Malawi, crafting custom-made and ready-made clothing for customers locally and internationally. From wedding gowns and corporate uniforms to traditional wear and everyday essentials — every piece is made with the same premium attention to detail.

Our goal is simple: to become Malawi's leading online tailoring and fashion platform, combining a simple, fast shopping experience with a genuinely premium fashion identity — proudly African, globally minded.
